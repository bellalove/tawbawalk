‘Tawba Walk

An Arts and Music Festival

The 'Tawba Walk Arts & Music Festival is back in action and bigger than ever before! Mark your calendars and get ready for an all-local experience unlike any other as we feature the area's top artists, bands, performance groups, food trucks, microbreweries, and much more!

Join us on Saturday, May 17th from 2-8 pm in Old Town Cornelius for a one-of-a-kind, community-inspired street festival that is sure to leave you feelin' a whole lotta love! The 'Tawba Walk is your all-access pass to discovering the top talent right here in your hometown while enjoying community as it should be. There will be fun for the whole entire family so bring the kids, your neighbors, and even your furry friends out to the event that's got everyone talking.... 'Tawba Walk VII!
